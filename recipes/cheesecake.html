Cheesecake!

About this recipe

Something to know if you want to fake a trip to New York and brag about it!

What do you need?

  • Philadelphia
  • Bookmark on The Cheesecake Factory website
  • Digestive Cookies
  • Butter
  • Sugar
  • The cat assistant from the previous recipe, who's silent but always there

Steps

  1. Grab the ingredients and talk to them, sweetly
  2. Abruptly smash the cookies like there's no tomorrow
  3. Mix them with butter and sugar (this is real, I actually love this recipe)
  4. Call someone to gently put the mix on the tray
  5. Pour the philadelphia cheese on the new york cheesecake cookie carpet
  6. Call the fake italian grandma from before to do the serious business: actually baking the cake
  7. Realize that you forgot the eggs so you didn't need to put the cake in the oven at all
  8. Enjoy your cute messiness!
