Lasagna!

About this recipe

Something to know if you want to fake a trip to Italy and brag about it!

What do you need?

  • Tomato sauce
  • Fake italian Grandma
  • Besciamella
  • Cat assistant
  • Mandolino
  • San Gennaro's protection

Steps

  1. Grab your grandma and pretend she's italian
  2. Give her a nice mandolino to make it believable
  3. Be a nice grandson/grandaughter and go to the grocery store to buy the ingredients
  4. Give the ingredients to your grandma
  5. When she starts making lots of hand gestures, she'll be ready to cook
  6. Let her do all the work
  7. When the lasagna is ready, call you friends and tell them your learnt how to make lasagna during your last fake trip to Italy.
  8. Enjoy!
